The Chip Shoratage

Sep. 16 2021

As most of you know, the chip shortage is far from over. New reports are indicating that the shortage could last until 2023. This is not only disheartening for gamers, as the chip shoratge has lead to shortages for graphics cards, but for a multitude of busniesses. In fact the car industry has been the one who was hit the hardest in all of this.
