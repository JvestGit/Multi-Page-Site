The new iPhone 13

Sep. 18 2021

Apple came in strong with their announcment of the new iPhone 13. While I am personally not a Apple fan, this is an exciting shake up in the moblie community. I am not really excited to be frank, and I have seen that many people online are a bit annoyed that this is basically the same design as the iPhone 12 and iPhone 11. The only really big improvement I have seen is that finally the iPhone 13 Pro will offer a 120hz refresh rate on their phones.
